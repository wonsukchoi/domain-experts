# ESCO expansion backlog

Candidate roles from the EU's [ESCO taxonomy](https://esco.ec.europa.eu/en) (v1.2, 2942 occupations, fetched 2026-07-15 via the public ESCO API) that have no counterpart among this repo's O*NET-derived roles.

**How this list was made:** all ESCO member occupations were diffed against the `roles/` directory by normalized title/alt-label match (409 exact + 518 fuzzy matches), then the remaining 1,994 candidates were semantically triaged: **702 duplicates** of existing roles, **1032 niche variants** (real but a parent role covers ~90% of the judgment — e.g. ESCO's 35 per-commodity "import export manager in X" variants), and the **260 genuinely new roles** below. Full per-occupation verdicts live in [`data/esco_occupations.tsv`](./data/esco_occupations.tsv).

Same rules as [ROADMAP.md](./ROADMAP.md): claim a role via PR, follow [AUTHORING.md](./AUTHORING.md). Checkbox = drafted.
