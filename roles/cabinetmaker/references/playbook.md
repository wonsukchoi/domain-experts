# Playbook

## Seasonal wood-movement calculation (filled example)

Solid walnut tabletop panel, 30" wide. Practical shop movement figure for walnut across a full seasonal humidity swing: ~1/4" per foot of width.

| Step | Value |
|---|---|
| Panel width | 30" = 2.5 ft |
| Movement figure (walnut, practical shop estimate) | 0.25"/ft |
| Expected total seasonal movement | 2.5 ft × 0.25"/ft = 0.625" |
| Groove depth allowance (movement + margin) | 0.625" + 0.125" margin = 0.75" (3/4") |

**Comparison across species (same 30" panel width, illustrative practical shop figures):**

| Species | Movement figure | Expected total movement |
|---|---|---|
| Walnut | 0.25"/ft | 2.5 × 0.25 = 0.625" |
| Quarter-sawn white oak | 0.15"/ft | 2.5 × 0.15 = 0.375" |
| Flat-sawn white oak | 0.30"/ft | 2.5 × 0.30 = 0.750" |

Same species, same width, but flat-sawn oak moves roughly double the quarter-sawn cut — groove and floating-panel allowances have to account for the actual cut, not just the species name.

## Floating-panel groove sizing worksheet (filled example)

30" walnut panel, calculated movement 0.625", built into a frame with a center-glued spline.

| Element | Dimension |
|---|---|
| Panel thickness | 3/4" |
| Groove width (into which panel edge fits) | 3/8" |
| Groove depth (frame side) | 1/2" minimum insertion |
| Total clearance built into groove for movement | 3/4" (covers the 0.625" calculated movement plus margin) |
| Glue point | Center of panel only, via a loose spline or single glued point — not along the side edges |

**Assembly note:** Panel edges on the left/right (cross-grain direction) sit in the groove with clearance for the full 0.625" of calculated movement; the top/bottom edges (running with the grain, minimal movement in that direction) may be glued more conservatively since movement along the grain is negligible by comparison.

## Moisture content acclimation log (filled example)

Shop EMC: 8%. Incoming walnut lumber delivery moisture readings:

| Board | Moisture reading on delivery | Difference from shop EMC | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| Board 1 | 8.2% | +0.2% | Ready to mill |
| Board 2 | 11.5% | +3.5% | **Acclimate in shop before milling — exceeds 2-point threshold** |
| Board 3 | 8.0% | 0% | Ready to mill |

Board 2 gets stickered and stored in the shop environment until its moisture reading comes within roughly a point or two of the 8% shop EMC before it's milled or assembled into any glued joint.
