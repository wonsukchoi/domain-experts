## 4. Financial sustainability check (filled example, quality investment)

| Investment | Annual cost | Funding source | Sustainable? |
|---|---|---|---|
| Lead teacher wage increase | $56,160 gross ($36,960 net of turnover savings) | Permanent $2.14/month tuition increase | Yes — recurring revenue source matches recurring cost |
| New outdoor play equipment | $22,000 one-time | Reserve fund draw-down | Yes — one-time cost matched to one-time funding source |
| Additional curriculum specialist (hypothetical, declined) | $58,000/year | Would require drawing down operating reserve indefinitely | No — declined pending a specific recurring revenue source |

**Rule applied:** recurring costs are matched to recurring, sustainable revenue sources; one-time costs may draw from reserves, but a recurring cost with no identified recurring funding source is not approved.
