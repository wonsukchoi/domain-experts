## UTG survey table (filled example)

Vessel: MV *Handysize 32*, bulk carrier, built 2011, classed ABS. Structural member: bottom shell plating, strake B, amidships. As-built thickness: 16.0 mm. ABS rule minimum (renewal) thickness for this scantling: 12.4 mm (allowable diminution 22.5%).

**Initial 5-point spot gauging (port side):**

| Point | Location | Reading (mm) | Diminution | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 3 m fwd of frame 62 | 13.9 | 13.1% | Pass |
| 2 | Frame 62 | 13.6 | 15.0% | Pass |
| 3 | 3 m aft of frame 62 | 12.9 | 19.4% | Pass, approaching allowance |
| 4 | 6 m aft of frame 62 | 11.9 | 25.6% | **Fail — below 12.4 mm rule minimum** |
| 5 | 9 m aft of frame 62 | 13.8 | 13.8% | Pass |

Average: 13.22 mm (17.4% diminution) — passes on average; Point 4 fails independently.

**Extended 9-point grid around Point 4 (1 m radius, once a below-minimum reading is confirmed):**

| Grid point | Offset from Point 4 | Reading (mm) | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| 4a | +0.5 m fwd | 13.5 | Pass |
| 4b | centerline, +0.3 m fwd | 12.6 | Pass, marginal |
| 4c | Point 4 (re-read) | 11.8 | Fail |
| 4d | +0.3 m aft | 11.6 | Fail |
| 4e | +0.6 m aft | 12.1 | Fail |
| 4f | +0.5 m outboard | 13.2 | Pass |
| 4g | +0.5 m inboard | 12.8 | Pass |
| 4h | +0.6 m fwd, +0.3 m outboard | 13.4 | Pass |
| 4i | +0.6 m aft, +0.3 m inboard | 12.0 | Fail |

Failing points (4c, 4d, 4e, 4i) define a pitted area of approximately 1.2 m × 1.0 m. Rule applied: repair area is drawn from the extended grid's failing points, not from Point 4 alone — the single original reading only triggers the grid, it doesn't size the repair.

## Condition & valuation survey report excerpt (filled example)

**Report type:** Pre-purchase condition survey
**Vessel:** MV *Handysize 32* — Bulk carrier, 32,000 DWT, built 2011, classed ABS
**Commissioned by:** Prospective buyer, for sale & purchase negotiation

> **1. Class and statutory status.** Class certificates current on face; class status report shows Special Survey No. 3 (due within current window) not yet completed by current owner. No open recommendations or conditions of class on file as of survey date. Statutory certificates (SOLAS, Load Line) current; no freeboard non-conformity observed at time of draft inspection.
>
> **2. Hull structure — bottom shell, strake B, amidships (port).** Five-point spot UTG: average 13.22 mm (17.4% diminution) against 16.0 mm as-built and a 22.5% allowable wastage — passes on average. Point 4 read 11.9 mm, below the 12.4 mm ABS rule minimum. Extended 9-point grid confirms a pitted area of approximately 1.2 m × 1.0 m. **Finding: local renewal required**, not general wastage — remainder of strake B satisfactory, next-survey monitoring only.
>
> **3. Recommendation — structural.** Local steel insert, 1.5 m × 1.2 m at as-built (16.0 mm) thickness, at the identified location. Estimated cost $12,000–$18,000 fully installed, to be completed at next drydocking.
>
> **4. Recommendation — transactional.** Because Special Survey No. 3 remains open, no independent class verification of hull condition beyond this survey's limited spot-check scope exists on the current cycle. Recommend the purchase agreement condition closing on, or hold in escrow not less than **$50,000**, pending seller's completion of Special Survey No. 3 and disclosure of its findings — sized above the one known repair to cover the realistic range of additional items a full tank-by-tank special survey gauging could surface that this narrower pre-purchase scope did not reach.
>
> **5. Overall condition rating:** Satisfactory, subject to items 3 and 4 above.
