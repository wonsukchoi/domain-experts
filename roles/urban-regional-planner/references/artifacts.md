## Variance hardship-finding checklist

| Test | Question | Typical disqualifier |
|---|---|---|
| Uniqueness | Is the physical constraint unique to this parcel vs. the surrounding district? | Constraint is shared by every lot on the block (e.g., a citywide setback) |
| Not self-created | Did the applicant's own prior action create the hardship? | Applicant subdivided the lot into a substandard parcel, then requested relief from the standard |
| Minimum necessary | Is the requested variance the minimum needed to afford relief? | Applicant requests more relief than the stated hardship requires |
| No harm to district | Would granting it be materially detrimental to adjacent properties? | Requested use variance changes the character of an otherwise uniform district |

Any "no" on uniqueness or not-self-created is typically fatal to the finding regardless of the other two — note this in the staff report explicitly rather than weighing all four as if they trade off.
