# Nursing Professor Playbook

Filled templates for the four recurring deliverables: clinical-judgment test blueprint, clinical-ratio compliance check, NCLEX pass-rate root-cause analysis, and SPEP corrective-action mapping.

## 1. Clinical-judgment test blueprint (NCJMM-aligned)

Build every high-stakes exam against the six NCSBN cognitive-skill layers, not just NCLEX content categories. Target distribution for a 75-item Med-Surg II midterm:

| NCJMM layer | Target % of items | Items (of 75) | Sample stem type |
|---|---|---|---|
| Recognize cues | 15% | 11 | "Which finding requires immediate follow-up?" |
| Analyze cues | 20% | 15 | "Which two findings together suggest...?" (select-all/bowtie) |
| Prioritize hypotheses | 15% | 11 | Rank three possible causes by likelihood |
| Generate solutions | 20% | 15 | Matrix/grid selecting appropriate vs. contraindicated actions |
| Take action | 20% | 15 | "Which action should the nurse take first?" |
| Evaluate outcomes | 10% | 8 | "Which finding indicates the intervention was effective?" |

After administration, run item analysis before finalizing scores:

| Item # | Difficulty (p-value) | Point-biserial | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| 14 | 0.31 | 0.09 | Flag — low discrimination, review for ambiguous distractor before scoring |
| 27 | 0.88 | 0.41 | Keep — appropriately easy, discriminates well |
| 41 | 0.22 | -0.05 | Pull from scoring — negative discrimination, likely miskeyed or flawed stem |

Rule of thumb: any item with point-biserial below 0.15 gets a content-expert review before the grade posts, regardless of difficulty.

## 2. Clinical ratio compliance calculation

Run this before finalizing each term's clinical schedule, not after a complaint:

| Course | Clinical site | Enrolled students | Instructors assigned | Ratio | State cap | Compliant? |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| NURS 302 Adult Health | Riverside Med-Surg | 20 | 2 | 1:10 | 1:10 | Yes — at cap, no buffer for absence coverage |
| NURS 310 OB | Community Birth Center | 16 | 2 | 1:8 | 1:8 | Yes |
| NURS 315 Peds | Children's Outpatient | 22 | 2 | 1:11 | 1:10 | **No** — split into 3 groups or add 1 instructor before term start |
| NURS 320 Psych | State Behavioral Health | 18 | 2 | 1:9 | 1:10 | Yes |

For the non-compliant row: cost of adding one adjunct clinical instructor for one 8-week rotation (≈$3,800) versus cost of invalidated clinical hours for 22 students if audited (full rotation re-do, ≈$0 direct cost but a semester's enrollment delay and an accreditor-reportable incident) — add the instructor.

## 3. NCLEX pass-rate root-cause template

Disaggregation table (fill before writing any narrative to the Board):

| Cut | N tested | N passed | Pass rate | Prior-year rate | Delta |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Program-wide | 68 | 54 | 79.4% | 91.3% | -11.9 pts |
| Campus A | 40 | 35 | 87.5% | 89.7% | -2.2 pts |
| Campus B | 28 | 19 | 67.9% | 93.1% | -25.2 pts |

If one cut's delta is more than roughly 3x the program-wide delta, treat it as the driver, not a contributor. Then cross-reference against the leading indicator collected before boards were sat:

| Cut | Mean ATI Comp. Predictor | Predicted pass rate | Actual pass rate | Predictor accurate? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Campus A | 78th percentile | 92% | 87.5% | Yes, within 5 pts |
| Campus B | 41st percentile | 65% | 67.9% | Yes, within 3 pts |

When predicted and actual pass rates agree within roughly 5 points, the predictive exam is doing its job and the root cause search should focus on inputs to that cohort (staffing, curriculum, site) during the terms leading up to it — not on the exam itself.

## 4. SPEP corrective-action mapping (accreditor-facing)

One row per non-compliant or at-risk standard, updated every review period — never assembled cold before a site visit:

| Standard | Status | Evidence | Corrective action | Date implemented | Verified by |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| III-D: Curriculum reflects clinical judgment expectations of licensure exam | Non-compliant, cycle 1 | Item analysis shows main-campus course exams still >60% recall-level items | Re-wrote 3 course blueprints to NCJMM layer targets (see Section 1) | Fall 2025 | Curriculum Committee, Oct 2025 minutes |
| IV-B: Program maintains adequate clinical resources for enrollment | At risk | Satellite clinical ratio 1:12 for 2 of 3 terms | Hired 2 adjunct clinical faculty; ratio now 1:10 | Spring 2026 | Clinical Coordinator sign-off, schedule audit |
| I-C: Substantive changes reported to accreditor prior to implementation | Non-compliant, cycle 1 | Satellite campus opened without prior substantive-change filing | Filed retroactive substantive-change report; new-site policy now routes through Dean's office before enrollment opens | Filed Jan 2026 | Accreditation liaison confirmation letter |

A standard with a "Corrective action" cell but no "Date implemented" or "Verified by" entry is not evidence of compliance — it is a plan, and reviewers score it as unaddressed.
