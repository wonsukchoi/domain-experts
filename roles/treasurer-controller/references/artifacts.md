## 2. Liquidity ladder model (filled example)

| Tier | Instrument | Amount | Access speed | Yield |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 — operating cash | Checking/money market | $6M (6 weeks of operating expenses) | Immediate | 0.5% |
| Tier 2 — short-term reserve | 30-90 day T-bills / CDs | $4M | 1-90 days | 4.8% |
| Tier 3 — longer-term investment | 6-12 month instruments, laddered to forecasted need dates | $2M | 6-12 months | 5.1% |

**Rule applied:** each tier is sized against actual forecasted need timing, not an arbitrary split — Tier 1 covers confirmed near-term obligations, not a guess.
