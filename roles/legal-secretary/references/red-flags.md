# Legal Secretary — Red Flags

### A name on a new-matter intake form partially matches an existing client or adverse party
- **Usually means:** either a coincidental name overlap (different entity, same or similar name) or a genuine conflict requiring attorney review — cannot be resolved by the person running the check alone.
- **First question:** is this the same legal entity/person, or a different one with a similar name — checked against a secondary identifier (address, EIN, matter number), not name alone?
- **Data to pull:** the firm's conflicts database entry for the matched name, including matter status (open/closed), matter type, and the attorney of record on that matter.

### An e-filing is rejected within 2 hours of the deadline
- **Usually means:** a technical formatting issue (PDF/A non-compliance, size limit, missing signature block) rather than a substantive problem with the document.
- **First question:** what is the exact rejection reason stated by the system or clerk's office — not a guess based on past rejections?
- **Data to pull:** the system's rejection notice text and the court's current technical filing requirements page.

### A local rule or standing order hasn't been re-checked in over 6 months for a court the firm files in regularly
- **Usually means:** the firm's template may be stale — courts and individual judges update formatting requirements without a firm-wide announcement.
- **First question:** when was this court's local rules and the assigned judge's standing order last pulled fresh, versus reused from the firm's template library?
- **Data to pull:** the court's published local rules (dated) and the specific judge's current standing order, if one exists.

### A multi-party event (deposition, hearing, mediation) has only one or two participants confirmed
- **Usually means:** the schedule was anchored on the easiest-to-move participant rather than the hardest, and is at risk of collapsing when a harder constraint surfaces.
- **First question:** which participant in this event has the narrowest availability window, and has that window been confirmed first?
- **Data to pull:** the availability windows already collected from each required participant, ranked by how many alternative dates each one offered.

### A deadline or confirmed date exists only in a personal note, not the firm's calendaring/practice-management system
- **Usually means:** a same-day interruption prevented the entry from being logged, and the deadline is now invisible to anyone else at the firm.
- **First question:** is this entered in the system of record right now, before doing anything else?
- **Data to pull:** the practice-management system's current entry (or lack of one) for this matter and date.

### A conflicts check comes back "no match" but the intake form is missing a required field (e.g., related entities, key witnesses)
- **Usually means:** the check was run against an incomplete name list, which understates the real conflicts search.
- **First question:** does the intake form list every party category the firm's conflicts policy requires (client, adverse party, related entities, known witnesses, referring party)?
- **Data to pull:** the firm's intake-form template's required-fields list, compared against what was actually filled in on this form.

### An attorney asks to "just clear" a possible conflict without a documented determination
- **Usually means:** time pressure is pushing toward skipping the Rule 1.7/1.9 documentation step, which is the exact scenario that creates malpractice exposure later.
- **First question:** is there a written determination on file, even a one-line one, or just a verbal "it's fine"?
- **Data to pull:** the conflicts database's documentation field for this matter — empty or populated.

### A rejected e-filing gets resubmitted without confirming the specific rejection reason was fixed
- **Usually means:** the resubmission addresses a guessed cause rather than the stated one, risking a second rejection with even less time left.
- **First question:** does the resubmission fix match the system's literal rejection-reason text?
- **Data to pull:** the original rejection notice, side by side with what changed in the resubmission.
