| Childcare partnership | Logistics (15) | $15,000/year | 65% | 5 |
| (No intervention) | Academic (12) | — | 0% | 12 |
| **Total** | | **$46,000/year** | | **42** |

**Resulting completion rate:** (200 − 42) ÷ 200 = **79%**, versus 58% baseline and 75% target. Cost per additional completion: $46,000 ÷ 42 = **$1,095**.

## 4. Outcome validation summary (filled example, for participant-facing claims)

| Claim used in recruitment | Supporting data | Data currency |
|---|---|---|
| "85% job placement within 6 months" | Actual: 71% for the most recent completed cohort (verified via employer/alumni follow-up) | Cohort completed 5 months ago — claim needs correction to match current data |
| "Average starting wage $19.50/hour" | Actual: $18.75/hour median for verified placements in the last 12 months | Current — matches |

**Rule applied:** every outcome claim used in recruitment materials is checked against current verified data before publication, not carried forward from a prior year's more favorable numbers.
