## 2. Moves management plan (filled example)

| Move # | Action | Timing | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Introductory meeting, no ask | Month 1 | Understand motivations, giving history, interests |
| 2 | Program site visit/tour | Month 3 | Build concrete connection to mission and outcomes |
| 3 | Personalized impact update (unrelated to any ask) | Month 5 | Demonstrate stewardship value before any solicitation |
| 4 | Case-for-support conversation | Month 7 | Introduce specific opportunity aligned to donor's stated interests |
| 5 | Formal solicitation | Month 9 | Ask, sized and framed based on prior conversations |
| 6 | Stewardship follow-up regardless of outcome | Month 10-11 | Report back, maintain relationship for future cultivation |

**Rule applied:** the ask (move 5) doesn't happen until 4 prior cultivation touchpoints have established genuine relationship and mission alignment — not scheduled by fundraising-calendar convenience alone.

## 3. Donor segmentation / pyramid (filled example)

| Tier | Annual giving capacity | Count in database | Cultivation model |
|---|---|---|---|
| Principal gifts | $100,000+ | 8 | Individualized moves management, board/ED involvement |
| Major gifts | $10,000-$99,999 | 45 | Individualized moves management, development officer assigned |
| Mid-level | $1,000-$9,999 | 210 | Semi-personalized outreach, portfolio-based |
| Annual/broad-based | Under $1,000 | 3,400 | Systematized campaigns, digital/direct mail |

**Rule applied:** cultivation time is allocated disproportionately to the principal and major tiers, which represent 53 donors but typically account for the large majority of total revenue — not spread evenly across the full donor base.

## 4. Stewardship calendar (filled example)

| Donor tier | Stewardship touchpoint | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Principal gifts | Personalized impact report + in-person update | Twice per year minimum |
| Major gifts | Personalized impact report | Once per year, timed before next solicitation |
| Mid-level | Segmented impact email with specific program outcomes | Twice per year |
| Annual/broad-based | Annual report + campaign-specific impact summary | Once per year |

**Rule applied:** every gift tier has a scheduled stewardship touchpoint before the next planned solicitation cycle — no donor reaches a re-ask without having first heard what their previous gift accomplished.
