## 3. Amendment negotiation summary (filled example)

| Version | Rebate/household | Fund diversion | Statewide shortfall created | Local benefit |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Original bill | $800 | $36M/year | $56M/year | $36,000,000/year |
| Negotiated amendment | $333 | $15M/year | $35M/year (partially offset by required $15M match) | $15,000,000/year |

**Rule applied:** the amendment trades a smaller, honestly-communicated local benefit for a proportionally reduced externalized cost — used as the price of a needed vote, not accepted or rejected as an all-or-nothing choice on the original text.
