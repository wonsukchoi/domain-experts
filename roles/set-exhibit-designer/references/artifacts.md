## Rigging plot (excerpt)

| Grid 12 | Zone 2 projector mount | 45 lbs | 250 lbs | 82% margin |

Rule applied: never load a rigging point above 60% of its rated capacity on a touring show — venue-to-venue rating variance and repeated load/unload cycles erode the safety margin faster than a single-install rating assumption accounts for.
