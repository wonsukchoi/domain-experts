## Same-family substitution table (spring/early-summer volatility window)

| Requested variety | Risk window | Same-family backup | Fallback if backup also unavailable |
|---|---|---|---|
| Peony (local, April-June) | Weather-dependent bloom window; can close early | Garden rose (large, ruffled cultivar) | Ranunculus, clustered for volume |
| Ranunculus | Short domestic season, imports variable | Anemone (similar cup form) | Lisianthus (double variety) |
| Lily of the valley | Extremely short season, high price volatility | Stephanotis (clustered, similar scent-forward role) | Sweet pea (seasonal, similar delicacy) |
| Dahlia (dinner-plate size) | Frost-sensitive, late-season only | Zinnia (large cultivar) | Garden rose, oversized bloom |
| Imported orchid (cattleya) | Air-freight delay risk | Domestic cymbidium | Phalaenopsis (smaller scale, adjust stem count up) |

## Processing and cold-chain log (filled example, delivery day)

| Time in | Variety | Action | Storage temp | Vase-life clock starts |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 7:10 AM | Garden roses (48 stems) | Re-cut underwater, into preservative solution | 36°F | 7:10 AM |
| 7:25 AM | Ranunculus (60 stems) | Re-cut underwater, into preservative solution | 36°F | 7:25 AM |
| 7:40 AM | Dahlias (30 stems) | Re-cut underwater, into preservative solution | 38°F (dahlias bruise below 36°F) | 7:40 AM |
| 8:00 AM | Cymbidium orchids (12 stems) | Re-cut, into water only (no biocide sensitivity) | 52°F (chilling-injury risk below 50°F) | 8:00 AM |
| 8:15 AM | Eucalyptus/greenery (200 stems) | Re-cut, bulk-bucketed | 36°F | 8:15 AM — holds 5-7 days, lowest priority for same-day use |

Rule applied: dahlias and orchids are pulled first for design work (shortest safe hold time); eucalyptus is held back for the last build step since it tolerates the longest wait.

## Build-day timeline (filled example, 2 PM ceremony)

| Time | Task |
|---|---|
| Day -1, 2:00 PM | Build ceremony-arch greenery base and all centerpiece foundation/greenery (hardy stems only) |
| Day -1, 5:00 PM | Move built foundations into cooler overnight |
| Event day, 6:00 AM | Process all remaining perishable stems (dahlias, ranunculus, garden roses) on arrival |
| Event day, 7:30 AM | Build bridal and bridesmaid bouquets (most perishable, latest possible build) |
| Event day, 9:00 AM | Insert focal blooms into pre-built centerpieces and arch |
| Event day, 10:30 AM | Load and deliver to venue |
| Event day, 11:30 AM | On-site install: arch, centerpieces, welcome table |
| Event day, 8:30 PM (post-reception) | Strike: collect rented hard goods, leave client-owned florals |
