# Elementary School Teacher — Playbook

## Running record scoring sheet (filled example)

Passage: 180 words, grade 3 winter benchmark text.

| Metric | Formula | This student | Band |
|---|---|---|---|
| Time | — | 2:15 (135 sec) | — |
| Errors | count | 14 | — |
| WCPM | (words − errors) ÷ (seconds ÷ 60) | (180−14) ÷ 2.25 = 73.8 | Above 70 monitor cutoff |
| Accuracy | (words − errors) ÷ words × 100 | (180−14) ÷ 180 = 92.2% | Instructional (90–94%) |
| Self-corrections | count / errors+SC | 2 / 16 = 12.5% SC rate | Below 30% target — low self-monitoring |

**Seasonal WCPM benchmark reference (Hasbrouck & Tindal, grade 3, 50th percentile):** Fall 71, Winter 86, Spring 100. Monitor/lower cutoff typically set ~15–20 WCPM below the 50th-percentile figure.

## MTSS/RTI tier decision table

| Accuracy band | Miscue pattern | Tier | Minutes/week | Source of minutes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 95–100% (independent) | any | Tier 1 (core only) | 0 additional | — |
| 90–94% (instructional) | ≥50% one structural feature | Tier 2, skill-specific | 60 (3×20 min) | Center-rotation time |
| 90–94% (instructional) | scattered, low self-correction | Tier 2, self-monitoring strategy | 60 (3×20 min) | Center-rotation time |
| Below 90% (frustration) | any | Tier 3, referral to intervention team | 90–150 | Pulled from a specialist block, case-managed |
