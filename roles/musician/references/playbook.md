# Playbook

Filled templates a working musician actually uses, not descriptions of them. Adapt the numbers to the gig; keep the structure.

## 1. Gig offer evaluation worksheet (filled)

Run this before accepting or declining any paid date.

```
DATE: Sat 14th          CONFLICTS WITH: Option A (corporate, $3,200 flat)

OFFER TYPE:      [ ] Flat guarantee   [x] Door deal   [ ] Guarantee + bonus
BASE NUMBERS:    Recoup: $3,000 fixed costs to venue
                 Split: 15% of net (after recoup) to band
                 Projected draw (promoter's number): 500 @ $20 = $10,000 gross
VERIFIED DRAW DATA FOR THIS VENUE/SLOT?  [ ] Yes  [x] No — treat projection
                 as best case, not expected case
DEPOSIT:         None offered — door deals rarely carry one
COMMISSION OWED: None (self-booked)
LOGISTICS:       120 min drive, no hotel, per diem $15/head, shared backline
                 (rider due 10 days out)
MERCH ALLOWED:   Yes, 20% venue cut
NAMED EXPOSURE BENEFIT: none confirmed — ask promoter directly before
                 counting this as a factor

BREAKEVEN DRAW (band nets $790/head to match Option A):
  Target total = $3,160 (4 x $790). Door alone must clear:
  $3,160 = 0.15 x (tickets x $20 - $3,000)  →  tickets ≈ 1,203
  That draw is more than double the promoter's own 500-ticket projection —
  door deal cannot match the guaranteed option even at the promoter's
  best case. Decision: decline or counter for a guarantee floor.
```

## 2. Deposit and contract terms by client type

| Client type | Deposit | Cancellation clause | Payment timing |
|---|---|---|---|
| New private/corporate client | 50% nonrefundable at signing | Client forfeits deposit inside 14 days of event | Balance due day-of, cash/check |
| Repeat private/corporate client (3+ prior bookings) | Optional — case by case | Same 14-day clause, waivable | Net-15 invoice acceptable |
| Venue guarantee (club/bar) | None standard | Band gets 50% of guarantee if promoter cancels inside 7 days | Night-of, cash or same-day transfer |
| Door/percentage deal | None (nothing to deposit against) | No-cancellation-fee norm; get load-in/set-time in writing regardless | Settled night-of against signed box-office count |
| Wedding/one-off private event | 50% nonrefundable, second 25% due 30 days out | Full remaining balance forfeited inside 30 days | Final 25% due day-of |

## 3. Split sheet (filled example)

Filled and signed the day a song is finished — before demoing, before release.

```
SONG: "Low Tide"                    DATE WRITTEN: 3/12
SESSION PRESENT: A. Reyes, M. Ojeda, T. Park

WRITER                | ROLE CONTRIBUTED           | SPLIT %
-----------------------|----------------------------|--------
A. Reyes               | Lyrics, melody             | 45%
M. Ojeda                | Chord progression, bridge  | 35%
T. Park                 | Arrangement, hook rewrite  | 20%
                                            TOTAL:     100%

PUBLISHING: each writer self-publishes their share (no co-publishing deal)
PRO REGISTRATION: each writer registers their % with their own PRO
                  (A. Reyes — ASCAP; M. Ojeda, T. Park — BMI)
SIGNATURES: [all three, dated same day]
```

**Rule:** if a fourth collaborator contributes later (a session player adding a hook), the sheet gets amended and re-signed before that version is released — never assumed retroactively.

## 4. Technical rider excerpt (filled, club-level date)

```
STAGE PLOT: 20' x 16' minimum. See attached diagram — DL vocal, DR guitar,
  USR drums, USC bass, keys stage right of drums.

BACKLINE NEEDED FROM VENUE (if not self-provided):
  - Drum kit: 22" kick, std 5-pc, working hardware, throne
  - Bass amp: 300w+ combo or head/cab, DI-capable
  - 2x guitar amps: combo, 1x12 or 2x12, no modeling-only substitutes
    without advance approval

INPUT LIST: 12 channels (kick, snare, OH L/R, bass DI, gtr1, gtr2,
  keys L/R, lead vox, bgv1, bgv2)

MONITORS: 4 mixes minimum (drums, bass/gtr1, gtr2/keys, vocals)

SOUNDCHECK: 45 min minimum, load-in 2 hrs before doors

HOSPITALITY: bottled water x6, 1 hot meal or buyout ($15/head) if set
  starts after 8pm
```

## 5. Deliberate practice log (filled)

Logs the failure point and the fix, not just minutes played.

```
DATE: 4/2   PIECE: "Low Tide" bridge, bars 32-36
FAILURE MODE: chord change bar 34 (Bbmaj7 -> Ebm) rushed, buzzed on
  the transition every take at performance tempo (132 bpm)

DRILL: isolated bars 32-36 only, metronome at 90 bpm (68% of target)
  - 10 clean reps at 90 -> stepped to 100 -> 110 -> 120 -> 132
  - stopped at first buzz each tempo step, dropped back 10 bpm, re-cleared

RESULT: clean at 132 for 8/10 reps by end of session (up from 2/10
  at session start). NEXT SESSION: recheck cold before assuming solved;
  add into full run-through only after 2 consecutive clean cold starts.
```

**Rule:** a passage only "graduates" to full-run-through practice after two consecutive clean cold starts on a different day — one good session doesn't confirm it's learned.

## 6. Merch settlement sheet (filled, night-of)

```
SHOW: Sat 14th, [Venue]              SETTLED BY: T. Park + venue mgr (both sign)

ITEM        START COUNT   END COUNT   SOLD   PRICE   GROSS
T-shirt          60           20       40    $25     $1,000
Vinyl LP          20           14        6    $30     $180
Stickers         100           55       45    $3      $135
                                              TOTAL:   $1,315

VENUE CUT (20%):                                       -$263.00
COST OF GOODS (40 shirts @ $8, 6 LPs @ $12, 45 stk @ $0.50): -$414.50
NET MERCH PROFIT:                                       $637.50

SIGNATURES: [band rep] ____________  [venue rep] ____________
```

**Rule:** counts happen twice — once at load-in (start count witnessed by both parties) and once at settlement (end count witnessed by both) — a single end-of-night count with no starting baseline can't catch shrinkage.
