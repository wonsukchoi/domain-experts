## 4. Explicit scope-cut communication (filled example)

> **What we're cutting:** Regional events program, reduced from $650K to $200K this year.
> **Why:** Reallocating toward paid search and content/SEO, which have measurably higher pipeline ROI (4.2x and 3.8x vs. events' 2.1x), to help close the gap toward this year's pipeline target.
> **What this means:** Fewer regional events, reduced field marketing presence in 3 secondary markets. Primary markets and top-account ABM events are unaffected.
> **What we're asking leadership to know:** This is a deliberate tradeoff to hit the pipeline number, not a quiet under-delivery — flag now if the reduced regional presence is a bigger concern than the pipeline math suggests.
