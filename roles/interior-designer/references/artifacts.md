## Lighting / footcandle worksheet (filled example)

| Zone | Task | Target fc | Fixture plan | Calculated fc | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Reception desk | Guest check-in, forms | 50 | 6 pendants (890 lm eff.) | 42 | **Under — add 2 task fixtures** |
| Open-office desks | Computer/paper work | 50 | 40 recessed LED (145 lm/W) | 54 | Pass |
| Circulation corridor | Walking, wayfinding | 10-20 | Ambient from adjacent zones | 16 | Pass |
| Breakout lounge | Reading, informal meeting | 30 | 4 floor lamps + ambient | 28 | Pass (within 10% tolerance) |

Correction path when under target: add supplemental task fixtures to the specific under-lit zone first; only re-spec the full fixture package if the shortfall exceeds 20% of target or affects more than one zone.

## ADA clearance checklist (filled example, reception + corridor)

| Element | Requirement | As-designed | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| Reception counter turning radius | 60" diameter or T-shape clear floor space | 54" (planter obstruction) | **Fail — relocate planter** |
| Corridor width, main circulation | 44" min (commercial), 36" at pinch points | 48" typical, 36" at storage-closet pinch | Pass |
| Door clear width, accessible route | 32" min clear | 34" (36" nominal door) | Pass |
| Reception counter height, accessible section | 34" max, 30" min clear knee space | 34" counter, 27" knee clearance | Pass |
| Wall-mounted thermostat/control | Reach range 15"-48" (forward), 9"-54" (side) | 46" AFF | Pass |

Every "Pass" still gets the dimension recorded, not just the checkmark — a punch-list inspector re-measures, and a recorded number defends the design faster than a re-derivation on site.
