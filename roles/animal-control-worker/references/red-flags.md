# Animal Control Worker — Red Flags

### Rabies vector species bite reported but animal not captured within the first few hours
- **Usually means:** the exposure risk assessment now has to run on regional rabies prevalence and post-exposure prophylaxis guidance rather than direct testing of the animal.
- **First question:** was the animal captured, or is it still at large, and what's the current county/state RVS rabies surveillance rate?
- **Data to pull:** capture-attempt log, state health department rabies surveillance data for the species and county.

### Home quarantine approved for a dog or cat with 2+ prior bite incidents on file
- **Usually means:** the elevated-risk criteria requiring facility quarantine were not checked before approving home quarantine.
- **First question:** does the department's record system show a prior bite for this animal or owner address?
- **Data to pull:** full incident history for the animal and the owner's address.

### Euthanasia performed with no documented medical or behavioral justification against a written protocol
- **Usually means:** the decision was driven by kennel capacity rather than a documented criterion, which is a liability and public-records exposure independent of whether the outcome was reasonable.
- **First question:** what specific protocol criterion (medical prognosis, behavioral evaluation result) is cited in the animal's file?
- **Data to pull:** the animal's medical/behavioral evaluation record and the shelter's written euthanasia protocol.

### CO2 chamber or other conditionally-acceptable euthanasia method used on a neonate under 10 days old
- **Usually means:** the method-selection step skipped the AVMA age/species restriction; neonates are poikilothermic and resistant to CO2-induced unconsciousness, making the method inappropriate regardless of general facility policy.
- **First question:** what is the documented age of the animal, and does the facility's protocol list an age restriction for this method?
- **Data to pull:** the animal's intake record with estimated age, the facility's written euthanasia method protocol.

### Body condition score not documented before a cruelty-case removal
- **Usually means:** documentation priority was misordered — removal happened before the condition evidence that supports it was recorded.
- **First question:** is there a timestamped BCS score and photograph set predating the removal?
- **Data to pull:** scene photographs with timestamps, the completed BCS worksheet.

### Dangerous-dog hearing referral filed without an underlying bite report or medical record attached
- **Usually means:** the referral was made on reputation or a verbal complaint rather than a documented incident meeting the statutory element.
- **First question:** what specific dated incident and medical/witness documentation supports this referral?
- **Data to pull:** the original incident report, ER or veterinary documentation of the injury, witness statements.

### Quarantine clearance issued before day 10 of the observation period
- **Usually means:** the clock was miscounted or clearance was issued under owner pressure rather than the full observation window.
- **First question:** what is the exact start date/time of the bite, and has the full 10-day window elapsed?
- **Data to pull:** the quarantine agreement with start date, the inspection log for day 0 and day 10 checks.

### Repeat calls to the same address for loose or aggressive livestock with no citation issued
- **Usually means:** an escalation pattern is being handled as a series of isolated incidents rather than a compliance case.
- **First question:** how many documented calls to this address exist in the past 12 months, and were any of them cited?
- **Data to pull:** the address's full call history, any containment-improvement notices previously issued.

### Stray hold shortened because the animal "looked" unclaimed or abandoned
- **Usually means:** an appearance-based judgment substituted for the statutory hold clock, which is a procedural violation independent of whether the animal was in fact unclaimed.
- **First question:** what is the statutory hold period for this jurisdiction and species, and has it fully elapsed?
- **Data to pull:** intake date/time, the jurisdiction's stray-hold ordinance, any logged owner-contact attempts.
