# Photographer — Playbook

## Pre-shoot brief (filled example, commercial product shoot)

| Field | Entry |
|---|---|
| Deliverable | 20 final retouched images, product-on-model and flat-lay |
| Usage scope | Print + digital ad campaign, United States, 6-month term, category-exclusive |
| Shoot days | 2 |
| Locations | Studio (day 1, controlled light), rooftop exterior (day 2, golden hour + overcast backup) |
| Talent | 2 models, signed releases required before shoot start |
| High-risk moments | Rooftop exterior — weather-dependent, no reshoot day booked |
| Backup plan | If overcast on day 2, shoot flat-lay/studio setups instead and reschedule exterior for a confirmed clear-weather day |

Rule: every high-risk moment gets an explicit backup plan logged before the shoot, not improvised on the day.

## Shot list with coverage flags (filled excerpt)

| # | Setup | Priority | Coverage requirement | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Hero product shot, studio, white background | Critical | 3 exposures bracketed (0, -1, +1 stop) + 2 angles | Primary campaign image, zero tolerance for a blown-highlight retake |
| 2 | Model wearing product, 3/4 angle, studio | Critical | Minimum 15 frames across the setup | Facial expression/blink risk — need selection room |
| 3 | Flat-lay, product + accessories | Standard | 5 frames, single angle | Static subject, low failure risk |
| 4 | Model, rooftop exterior, golden hour | Critical | Full setup shot twice — once at golden hour, once with fill flash as weather hedge | Single-day, weather-dependent, no reshoot booked |
| 5 | Detail shots (fabric texture, stitching) | Standard | 3 frames per detail, macro lens | Low failure risk, easily reshot if needed |

## Usage-fee pricing grid (filled example)

| Multiplier category | Tier | Factor |
|---|---|---|
| Base rate (digital-only, regional, non-exclusive, 6-month) | — | $500/image |
| Medium | Digital only | ×1.0 |
| Medium | + Print | ×1.5 |
| Medium | + Broadcast/video | ×2.0 |
| Geography | Regional | ×1.0 |
| Geography | National | ×1.3 |
| Geography | Global | ×1.8 |
| Exclusivity | Non-exclusive | ×1.0 |
| Exclusivity | Category-exclusive | ×1.4 |
| Exclusivity | Full exclusive (no other buyer, any category) | ×2.0 |

Apply multipliers cumulatively (multiply, don't add) against the base rate, then multiply by image count for the total usage fee. Full buyout (unlimited duration/geography/medium/exclusivity) is quoted separately, not derived from this grid — it prices the loss of all future licensing revenue on the set, not a scoped use.

## Lighting-ratio reference (filled example)

| Ratio (key:fill) | Read | Typical use |
|---|---|---|
| 1:1 | Flat, shadowless | Product catalog, medical/ID photography |
| 2:1 | Soft, approachable | Corporate headshot, lifestyle |
| 4:1 | Moderate contrast, dimensional | Editorial portrait |
| 8:1+ | High contrast, dramatic | Fashion, mood-driven editorial |

Measure with an incident light meter at the subject: read key-light-only, then key+fill combined, and derive the ratio from the stop difference (each full stop doubles the ratio — a 2-stop difference between key-only and fill-only readings is a 4:1 ratio).
