# Dermatologist — Playbook

## Melanoma staging: margin and SLNB decision table (AJCC 8th ed. / NCCN v2 2024)

| Breslow depth | Ulceration | WLE margin | SLNB |
|---|---|---|---|
| In situ | n/a | 0.5–1cm | Not indicated |
| ≤0.8mm | Absent, mitotic rate <1/mm² | 1cm | Not routinely offered |
| ≤0.8mm | Present, or mitotic rate ≥1/mm² | 1cm | Discuss and offer |
| 0.8–1.0mm | Either | 1cm | Discuss and offer |
| 1.01–2.0mm | Either | 1–2cm | Discuss and offer |
| 2.01–4.0mm | Either | 2cm | Discuss and offer |
| >4.0mm | Either | 2cm | Discuss and offer |

Worked row: a 1.4mm, non-ulcerated lesion gets a 1–2cm margin (surgeon's choice within range, usually 1.5–2cm balancing cosmesis against local recurrence) and an SLNB discussion — depth alone above 1mm crosses the discuss-and-offer line regardless of ulceration or mitotic rate.

## Pigmented lesion triage: 7-point checklist (Argenziano)

Major criteria (2 points each): atypical pigment network, blue-white veil, atypical vascular pattern.
Minor criteria (1 point each): irregular streaks, irregular pigmentation, irregular dots/globules, regression structures.

**Scoring rule:** total ≥3 → excisional biopsy. Total <3 with strong clinical suspicion (rapid change, patient-reported bleeding) → biopsy anyway; the checklist raises sensitivity for excision decisions, it doesn't override a strong clinical read.

**Worked example:** lesion has an atypical pigment network (2) + irregular streaks (1) = 3. Crosses the threshold → excisional biopsy, not shave.

## Psoriasis severity-to-treatment ladder

| Step | Trigger | Therapy | Reassess |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | BSA <5%, no special sites | High-potency topical corticosteroid + vitamin D analog (e.g., calcipotriene) | 4–8 weeks |
| 2 | BSA <5% but face/scalp/palms/soles/genitals/intertriginous involved, OR step 1 fails | Site-appropriate topical + consider NB-UVB phototherapy | 8–12 weeks |
| 3 | BSA >10% or PASI >10, or step 2 fails | Conventional systemic (methotrexate, cyclosporine, or acitretin) | 12–16 weeks |
| 4 | Step 3 fails, or patient meets step-3 criteria and prefers targeted therapy | Biologic (IL-17 or IL-23 inhibitor) | Assess PASI75 at week 12 |

**Trial-endpoint benchmark:** modern IL-17/IL-23 biologics report PASI75 response around 80–90% at week 12 in pivotal trials, versus roughly 50–60% for older conventional systemics — a patient failing to hit even PASI50 by week 12–16 on a biologic is a treatment-failure signal, not a "give it more time" signal.

## Acne IGA (Investigator's Global Assessment) to treatment matrix

| IGA | Presentation | First-line |
|---|---|---|
| 0–1 (clear/almost clear) | Occasional comedones, rare papule | Topical retinoid ± benzoyl peroxide |
| 2 (mild) | Some comedones, few papules/pustules | Topical retinoid + topical/oral antibiotic combination |
| 3 (moderate) | Multiple papules/pustules, ≤1 small nodule | Oral antibiotic + topical retinoid + benzoyl peroxide, or hormonal therapy if applicable |
| 4 (severe) | Nodular/conglobate, scarring risk | Isotretinoin candidacy evaluation |

## iPLEDGE isotretinoin monitoring calendar (REMS)

| Visit | Timing | Required |
|---|---|---|
| Baseline | Day 0 | Two negative pregnancy tests (if capable of pregnancy), baseline LFT/lipids, contraception counseling (2 forms), enrollment confirmation |
| Month 1 | Day 30 (±7-day window) | Pregnancy test within 7 days before pickup, prescription pickup within 7-day authorization window |
| Month 2–6 | Monthly | Pregnancy test within 7-day window each cycle, LFT/lipid recheck at month 2 and as clinically indicated, cumulative dose tracking |
| End of course | Cumulative dose reached | Target cumulative dose 120–150mg/kg total to reduce relapse risk; confirm with final pregnancy test before discontinuation counseling |

**Worked example:** a 60kg patient targeting a 120mg/kg cumulative course needs 7,200mg total. At a stable maintenance dose of 40mg/day, that's 180 days (~6 months) of therapy — a course projected to finish in 3 months at that same daily dose has an inconsistent target and should be re-checked against the intended cumulative-dose goal, not just the daily dose.
