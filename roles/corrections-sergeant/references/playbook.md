# Corrections Sergeant — Playbook

## Staffing-floor reference by unit type (illustrative, facility-specific)

| Unit type | Census threshold | Floor minimum (housing floor) | Rover required? |
|---|---|---|---|
| General population, direct supervision | >100 inmates | 2 officers | Yes, unless suspended with logged compensating measure |
| General population, direct supervision | ≤100 inmates | 1 officer | No |
| Special management / segregation | Any | 2 officers | Yes — no exceptions, two-officer escort rule applies |
| Medical/mental-health watch housing | Any | 1 officer per active watch, plus floor minimum | No |
| Intake/booking | Any | 2 officers | No |

A floor minimum is a legal floor, not a staffing goal — any shift running below it needs either a mandate to restore it or a logged compensating measure with a stated reason, not silence.

## Early Warning/Intervention System (EIS) threshold table

| Trigger | Rolling window | Required action |
|---|---|---|
| 3 use-of-force incidents (any type) | 90 days | Documented supervisor review + coaching conversation before next shift |
| 2 sustained inmate complaints (any category) | 6 months | EIS review, notify to lieutenant, consider training referral |
| 4 sick-leave call-ins on a scheduled overtime/mandate day | 90 days | Attendance review, pattern flagged to scheduling |
| 1 PREA-relevant allegation (any outcome) | N/A — single occurrence | Immediate reassignment pending investigation outcome, EIS entry regardless of finding |

A threshold hit generates a required action, not an optional check-in — an EIS entry with no follow-up documented is functionally the same as no EIS at all in a later liability review.

## Progressive discipline matrix (filled example)

| Step | Trigger | Documentation required | Escalation if repeated |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. Verbal counseling | First minor policy violation (tardiness, uniform, minor procedural lapse) | Logged counseling form, officer signature or refusal noted | Same category within 6 months → Step 2 |
| 2. Written reprimand | Second occurrence of same category, or a moderate violation (unlogged post abandonment under 10 min, failure to complete required round) | Written reprimand in personnel file, officer acknowledgment | Same category within 12 months → Step 3 |
| 3. Suspension | Third occurrence, or a serious violation (falsified log entry, unauthorized use of force) | Formal investigation summary, suspension order with duration | Any recurrence → Step 4 |
| 4. Termination recommendation | Fourth occurrence in the ladder, or any single act warranting immediate separation (excessive force sustained by investigation, PREA violation sustained) | Full investigative file forwarded to command staff | N/A |

Apply the same ladder to the same misconduct category for every officer — the arbitration risk is disparate application, not the discipline level itself.
